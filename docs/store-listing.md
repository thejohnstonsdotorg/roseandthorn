## Long description (≤ 4000 chars)

```
Rose & Thorn turns dinner into a family ritual. One phone is passed around the table — each person shares their Rose (the highlight of their day) and their Thorn (a difficulty or lowlight). The app guides deeper reflection with warm follow-up prompts, then saves a history your family can look back on for years.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Set up your family in under a minute — add members and choose a fun emoji character for each person.
2. Tap "Begin Dinner" to start a session.
3. Pass the phone to the first person. They enter their Rose and respond to a deepening prompt, then their Thorn and another prompt.
4. The phone passes to the next person, and so on.
5. At the end, a summary celebrates everyone's contributions with a closing reflection.
6. Every session is saved locally and browsable by date.

FEATURES
• Pass-and-play: single device, large tap targets, minimal typing required
• Deepening prompts: curated follow-up questions that encourage real reflection — gratitude, generosity, curiosity, growth
• Generative artwork: every Rose & Thorn entry gets a unique procedural illustration, seeded from the member's name and entry text
• Emoji character avatars: each family member picks an emoji (🦊, 🐉, 🤖, …) that becomes their personal character in AI-generated artwork
• On-device AI artwork (opt-in): enable AI Images in Settings to generate Stable Diffusion illustrations for each entry — entirely on your phone, nothing sent to a server. Requires a one-time 1.9 GB model download.
• Session history: browse every past dinner by date with thumbnail artwork
• Export: share your family's complete history as a JSON file — import it on another phone to keep everyone in sync
• No ads, no subscriptions, no accounts, no passwords

PRIVACY FIRST
All data — family names, Rose & Thorn entries, session history, and generated artwork — is stored only on your device. Nothing is ever transmitted to us. See the full privacy policy for details.

OFFLINE FIRST
Rose & Thorn works entirely without internet. The only optional network use is the one-time model download for on-device AI artwork.

FAMILY RITUAL, SIMPLIFIED
Designed for the dinner table: warm amber colors, gentle haptics, and a flow that takes less than 10 minutes for a family of four.
```

(char count: ~1,960 — well under 4,000 limit; room to expand with future features)
