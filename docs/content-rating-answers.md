# IARC Content Rating Questionnaire — Rose & Thorn v1.0

Pre-filled answers for the Play Console Content Rating section.
Complete the IARC questionnaire in Play Console → Main store listing →
Content rating. Copy your answers from here.

Expected final rating: **Everyone** (ESRB) / **PEGI 3** / **IARC Everyone**

---

## Category selection

When Play asks "Which category best describes your app?", select:

> **Reference, News, Educational, or a Utility app**
> (closest to a family journaling / reflection utility)

## Notes

- The UGC question is the only one that might prompt a follow-up. Play will
  ask whether the UGC is "shared publicly or with unknown users." The answer
  is **No** — entries are private to the family using that one device. The
  export is a local share-sheet action, not a public post.
- Play Console may show a "parental controls" badge or similar tag because
  of the UGC declaration. This is fine and expected.
- When Cloud AI is re-enabled in v1.1, no new rating questions are triggered
  (image generation via BYO API key is a utility function, not content that
  changes the rating).
